Why Baseball?

Growing up around baseball and playing since I was 3 years old, it has shaped who I am far beyond just competition. Over the years, it has taught me discipline, confidence, and how to trust the people around me. This page highlights some of the lessons I have learned through the game and a few moments that represent my journey playing at BYU.

Lessons I've learned From Baseball

  1. Discipline
    • Showing up every day
    • Working hard even when tired
  2. Teamwork
    • Trusting others
    • Putting the team first
  3. Confidence
    • Learning from failure
    • Staying mentally strong
